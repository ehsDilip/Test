Order Information

Please Order Early!

All orders begin with your idea. You can call, fax or e-mail us your Idea and we will have our art department do a full color work-up for you. What you receive from us will be exactly what your pin will look like as finished product. When you receive the artwork you will need to callus with your approval or any changes.

If yourequire changes we will re-do the artwork and send It to you. When you are happy with the artwork call and give us your approval. There Is no need to return the artwork, we wil have full color copies as well.

When you callIn your approval we will then discuss sizes and prices. On your final confirmation we will send you acknowledgment of order. The whole process from beginning to finished product usually take 4 weeks.

DPH Pins believes in providing quality products to our customers. If you are unhappy with your order for any reason, DPH will refund or remake your order. All orders refunded or remade must be returned to DPH. NO EXCEPTIONS.
