Ordering Custom Pins

To get your quote, Call: 740.264.2450

General Info

Getting Artwork

All orders begin with your idea. What you receive from from us will be exactly what your pin will look like as a finished product. When you receive the artwork you then only need to call us with your approval or changes.

Getting Changes to Artwork

If you require changes we will redo the artwork and send it to you. When you are happy with the artwork call and give us your approval. There is no need to return the artwork, we will have full color copies as well.

Final Approval

When you call in your approval we will then discuss sizes and prices. On your final confirmation we will send you acknowledgment of your order. The whole process from beginning to finished product usually take 4 weeks. Plus our Iron Clad Guarantee. If you are unhappy with your order for any reason, simply return it for a full refund of we will remake the order at no charge.

General Ordering Info/Requirements

  • Minimum order of 150 pieces.
  • New Artwork requires a $50 non-refundable deposit which will be applied to the confirmed order
  • Die charges in advanced for samples.
  • Balance due before delivery
  • Please allow 4-6 weeks for delivery
